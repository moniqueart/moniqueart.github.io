Monique Bravo

I am a digital artist based in Winnipeg. I am currently a 4th year Fine Art student focusing on Graphic Design in the University of Manitoba. I mainly work on illustration and animation but I also have experience with design and video.

I worked at my local senior centre as a Videographer/Graphic Designer and helped them create their brand by making their organization logo and banners. I also helped increase their public engagement through videos that promote their organization, senior programs, and volunteer training.

Currently, I’m focusing on independent learning and creating self-projects in order to break into the animation industry.
