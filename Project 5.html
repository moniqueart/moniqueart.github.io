Fool's Gamble

Animation

This is a mostly looping animation combined with relaxing classical music(Gymnopedie No.1 and 3 by Erik Satie). This was inspired by the many lofi music videos with looping animation background in Youtube. I inserted a very subtle narrative into this based on Greek mythology where the ferryman Charon carries the souls of the dead across the river Styx into the Underworld.

Software used: Adobe Illustrator CC, Adobe Animate CC
