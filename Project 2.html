Into the Animation Universe

Imagined exhibition poster/brochure

This is a poster/brochure about an imagined exhbibition for the making of the film "Spider-man: Into the Spiderverse". It is made in such a way that as the brochure unfolds, the big poster will be revealed in the back and can be used as decorative.
For the overall design, I emulated the art style of the film inluding the vibrant colours, glitchy effects, and the Ben-Day dots as seen in comic books.

This is how the brochure will look like as it unfolds. You first open the cover, and unfold the flaps one by one to reveal the inner red panels.
